The Ultimate Guide To InboxDollars

 

Similar to Swagbucks, InboxDollars is a Get-Paid-To site which gives you the opportunity to earn money for completing surveys, watching videos and more.

 

InboxDollars might not look like much from first glance and with a $30 payout minimum, you might be put off.

But stick with me and in this guide I’ll show you how you can earn quick cash with InboxDollars.

Signing Up

You’ll need to sign up in order to start earning. Click here to create an account. Don’t worry, it’s free and you don’t need to enter any credit card information or other payment details.

You’ll instantly receive a $5 bonus in your account. This is yours, for free, to withdraw along with the rest of the cash you’ll have earned when you reach the payout threshold.

So now there’s only $25 left to earn! But how?

Surveys

A penny for your thoughts… or maybe even a few dollars.

Surveys are a popular way to make money online because they’re so easily accessible to pretty much anyone and, provided you already have internet access and a phone or laptop, it doesn’t cost you any money.

InboxDollars has its own survey router and pays you for every survey you successfully complete.

You’ll have to complete your profile though to really get anywhere. But that’s the same for all survey sites.

When I first joined, there weren’t many surveys available for me to take but once I started filling in my profile, I saw a huge increase in the number of surveys that were open to me.

This is because companies want the opinions of very specific groups of people and my profile tells that company that I’m in that specific group of people.

The amount of money you can earn with surveys on InboxDollars seems to be limited, with most surveys being around the 25¢ – 75¢ range.

That being said, there are sometimes surveys worth a bit more. $5.00 is the highest I’ve seen.

But although the incentive is on the lower side of the scale, I found that I was able to complete most of the surveys I was offered and not be disqualified halfway through.

Offers

Just like Swagbucks, Inboxdollars gives you access to a number of offerwalls.

Some of these offers include downloading apps, signing up for free trials or enrolling in a mailing list.

Not all offers are free though and some require “paid participation.” So be sure to read through the offer’s terms before completing it.

Cashback

You can earn cashback by shopping at your favorite online retailers with InboxDollars!

Many popular stores in the United States are included such as Target, Staples, The Home Depot and Walmart!

Simply click through to the retailer’s website from the InboxDollars cashback page, make your purchase as usual and your InboxDollars account will be credited with a percentage of your purchase as cashback.
