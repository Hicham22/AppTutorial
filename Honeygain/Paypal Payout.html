Honeygain Payout Payment Proof via Paypal

The entire withdrawal method is detailed in 8 simple steps with screenshots in this article.

Step 1: Request Payout

Log in to your HoneyGain Dashboard and click “Request Payout”. Ensure that you have reached a minimum payout of USD 20.

Click the “Request Payout” blue button to start the withdrawal process.

Step 2: Choose Payout Option

Choose PayPal as your withdrawal option. 

At this point of the Honeygain payout request, it does not matter if you have an existing Paypal account or not.

You can create a free Paypal account subsequently.

It also does not matter if your HoneyGain account’s email address is not the same as your Paypal account.

You will have to wait for an email from HoneyGain to confirm your Paypal email address via their payout provider Tipaltic. This will take about a day.

Choose “PayPal” to receive the Payout

Step 3: Confirm the Amount to Withdraw

Confirm the amount to withdraw by clicking “Request Payout”.

Confirm the Amount

Step 4: Register at the Tipalti website

You will receive an email from HoneyGain, requiring you to register and provide your Paypal email account to the Tipalti website, their payout provider.

Email from HoneyGain asking to register on Tipalti Portal.

Step 5: Provide your PayPal email address to Tipalti

Fill in your information on the Tipalti website and provide your Paypal email address. 

Once done, you will have to wait for approximately 1-2 working days for the amount to reach your PayPal account successfully.

Tipalti Payment Details Screen

Step 6: Wait for the Email to inform Payout is on the way

During this 1-2 days wait, you will receive an email from HoneyGain, telling you that the withdrawal process is on the way.

Email from HoneyGain informing that Payout is on the way.

Step 7: Wait for the Email to inform Payout is Successful

You will receive an email from HoneyGain that the transaction to your PayPal account is successful.

Email from HoneyGain confirming successful payout to Paypal

Step 8: Check your Paypal to confirm

Log in to your Paypal account to confirm your HoneyGain amount payout. 

You will see the sender is from payouts@honeygain.com

That’s it. Congratulations! 

You had successfully withdrawn your money from HoneyGain to your Paypal account.
