HoneyGain: Proof of Bitcoin payout

The steps to having a successful BTC payout are as follows;

1. Ensure that you meet the minimum payout amount

Login to your HoneyGain dashboard, and you can see your current balance.

If you meet the minimum payout amount of $20, the “Request Payout“ blue button will be made available to you.

Click on it to initiate the payout process.

2. Choose Bitcoin (BTC) as your preferred Payout Request

Choose “Bitcoin” in this next step.

3. Provide your Bitcoin wallet address

This is the part where you will enter your Bitcoin public wallet address.

For security and ease of use, you can get your own free Bitcoin (BTC) deposit wallet address at any of these exchanges & platforms;

  • Binance
  • Coinbase
  • Bittrex
  • Gate.IO

This is also commonly referred to as your wallet’s public keys or public address, and it is meant to be shared with those required for receiving or sending purposes.

Please note that you MUST NOT provide your Bitcoin Private Keys here.

Your private keys are only for yourself, and anyone who possesses it will have access to all your BTC inside.

You can use either one of these;

  • Cryptocurrency Exchange wallet address;
  • Software-based wallet address
  • Hardware-based wallet address
  • Paper-based wallet address

I will be using an exchange (Binance) wallet address in this particular payout.

4. Honeygain BTC Security Check

HoneyGain will send a verification code to your email inbox.

This is the email address that is registered with your current HoneyGain account.

Enter the code and proceed to the next step.

5. Payout Request Completed

You will see this message that says, “The equivalent of <your-payout-amount> in BTC will be sent in 2 business days.

6. Receiving BTC Payout

In 2 business days, you will receive the BTC amount in your wallet address.

Your cryptocurrency exchange service will first inform you via email if you are using their wallet.

Login to your wallet to confirm the deposit amount.

 

FAQ

How long does it take to withdraw BTC from Honeygain to the wallet address?

It will take exactly two (2) business days to see the BTC in your wallet address.
If the day of your BTC withdrawal request falls on a business day, it is counted as Day 1.

Business Day is defined as Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays.

You can use any BTC wallet of your choice, such as an exchange wallet address a software or hardware wallet address.

In this article, the request for BTC payout was made on Friday, 14 May 2021, at 7 am (UTC Time), and HoneyGain was deposited into the BTC wallet on Monday, 17 May 2021, 11:30 am (UTC Time). This is precisely 2 business days of processing.

How do I know if the BTC amount has been deposited into my wallet address?

If you are using a cryptocurrency exchange like Binance, it will inform you via email of every deposit that enters your account. Login to your exchange and confirm the amount.

If you use other software or hardware wallets, you will likely have to check once two (2) business days are up manually as there will be no notifications to you.
