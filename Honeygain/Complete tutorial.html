How Honeygain Works

Honeygain works by facilitating the connection between those who want to share their internet for money (users), and those who want to access the internet from residential IPs across the globe (clients).

When you download the Honeygain app onto your device and start sharing your internet, you’re adding yourself to Honeygain’s network as a proxy server. In other words, you’re turning your device into a gateway that Honeygain’s clients can access the internet through.

Clients pay Honeygain for access to the network in order to do location-based research related to:

  • Market research
  • Ad-fraud prevention
  • Brand protection
  • Pricing intelligence
  • Travel fare aggregation; and
  • SEO monitoring services

(As mentioned in the safety section, the network could be used for other things too. There’s really no way to be sure.)

As a user, you only get paid for the internet you actively share, so if there’s no demand for internet access in your location, you won’t be making any money.

In order to start sharing your internet and making money, you can download the app on a Windows, macOS, or Android device (Linux and iOS apps are in the works). After you’ve earned at least $20, you can cash out via PayPal or BTC.
