Earning with:

  • Normal bandwidth selling and content delivery

If you have some spare computers, phones and internet connections at your disposal, make full use of them: Honeygain uses 10% of your bandwidth per device (from my understanding) so having 2 devices on 1 IP lets Honeygain utilize 20% and also provides stability.
If you haven't maxed out your device limit and after 1-2 months of use the earnings are still high, then consider expanding your operations. Expand only after that amount of time as the first 1-2 weeks are when Honeygain tests your network with "dummy" data and thus could result in higher earnings. I've seen people go all in too early and then be mad that their earnings dropped drastically. Get some Android 6 and above phones and additional connections. If your country has unlimited data plans available for phones at a reasonable cost then you consider using those but of course a cable connection would be better as it tends to be more stable and faster.
In case of computers, if you want to run them 24/7 then make sure that the power cost is reasonable and can be offset with your earnings. Hook them up to your fastest connection(s) and if possible, turn on content delivery. It uses more bandwidth and pays you by the hour but in most cases it can earn you far more than the normal bandwidth service.
Installing the app on your close ones' devices is also an option (do that only with full consent and make sure they understand why!) but that can be unreliable earnings wise as the app could go down and you couldn't fix it right away.

  • Referrals

Referring can be a gold mine if you're good at marketing or have a lot influencer power over people. Just by checking HG's Discord you can see people who earn ~$0.20 per day from sharing bandwidth but $2-5 per day from referrals but these numbers require quite a bit of work. It isn't 2019 anymore (when HG launched) and simply dropping your referral link on money-making forums and subreddits won't get you tons of referrals but probably backlash instead because everyone knows about HG at this point.
This means that the two obvious routes to go are marketing and influencing. In both cases you'd need a niche where people would like to earn extra money but probably aren't familiar enough with beermoney community to know about Honeygain, for example, artists.
In case of marketing, you could try setting up ad campaigns on various platforms that catch artists' attention to, for example, recoup their supply costs. This requires quite a bit of knowledge of ad platforms and making ads that actually work + some money for advertising which can get costly at times.
- Becoming an online influencer isn't exactly a quick method either as you'd have to pick a platform, start making content that hooks people and then subtly plug Honeygain. If you have some influence in a group of people IRL (for example an art class) then you could do some mouth-to-mouth promotion there.
Again, there's the option of utilizing FFF (friends, family, fools) but I doubt there are a lot of you out there who have friends and family that are eagerly interested in making money online in ways they probably don't understand.

  • Honey Jar

Not much to say here, it's basically a daily bonus that has to be claimed manually. Do your best to claim it daily as even if you get only 10-20Cr per day, it can still earn you some extra.
