What is Lucky Pot

 

Lucky Pot is a game that is a part of the Honeygain experience and allows users to win up to 10 000  credits (which are worth $10) every day and reach the minimum payout threshold of $20 faster.

 

How to open Lucky Pot

 

Please note that the steps provided below are only for the web version of Honeyain Dashboard. If you are on Android, Lucky pot will pop-up automatically after you share 15 Mb of internet bandwidth.

 

To open Lucky Pot, you must share at least 15 Mb of internet bandwidth. Please note that you must claim Lucky Pot winnings the same day after you've shared 15 Mb of internet bandwidth. You can open Lucky Pot only once a day. If you meet these conditions, you should be able to see the "Open Lucky Pot" button on the right side of the Dashboard (if you are on the mobile version of the Dashboard, you should see the Lucky Pot icon at the very top of the Dashboard).

After you have opened the Luck Pot, you will see a popup with a button called "Open Lucky Pot". Click on it to open Lucky Pot and see how much you've won! 

After you click on "Open Lucky Pot" button, you will be presented with how much you've won. Credits will be added to your earnings momentarily. 

 

The Rules of Lucky Pot

 

Honeygain is happy to introduce the Lucky Pot game – a chance to win a random amount of free extra credits* every day, subject to eligibility conditions established below. All you need to do is open the Honeygain app and check whether today's your lucky day!

The rules of the Lucky Pot are as follows:

  • Only Active Users of Honeygain are eligible to participate in the Lucky Pot game. An Active User is any user who has generated a considerable amount of Shared Traffic during the previous calendar day. Please note that Honeygain reserves the right to, at all times and in its sole discretion, determine whether a particular user is as an Active User eligible to participate in the Lucky Pot game.
  • Each Active User can only participate in the Lucky Pot game once in a single calendar day. Please note that if an Active User does not participate in the Lucky Pot game on a given day, their chance to participate is not transferred to the following day!

* Credits are Honeygain network's digital assets, allocated to the users' accounts to compensate for their Shared Traffic. It has a value attributed to it by Honeygain.
